Kailun Yang

Since 2014, I’m a Ph.D. student in College of Optical Science and Engineering at Zhejiang University with the major in Instrument and Meter Engineering. I received the B.S. degree in Measurement and Control Technology and Instrument from Beijing Institute of Technology and the dual degree in Economics from Peking University.

Currently I’m a joint training Ph.D. student in Wangkaiwei Team working with Dr. Kaiwei Wang and Dr. Jian Bai at Zhejiang University, and Robesafe research group at University of Alcalá working with Dr. Luis Miguel Bergasa. Since 2016, I’ve been appointed as Chief Technology Officer at Hangzhou KR-VISION Technology Co., Ltd.

I’m interested in computer vision technology for the visually impaired. My main research over the past few years has been on stereo vision, pRGB-D sensor, sensor fusion, traversability awareness, obstacle avoidance and scene segmentation.
